Providence Community Health Center
Descripción
Escucha
Providence Community Health Centers (PCHC) es una organización de atención médica sin fines de lucro y el único centro de salud calificado federalmente (FQHC) en Providence, Rhode Island. PCHC brinda servicios de atención médica primaria de calidad que son asequibles, integrales y culturalmente sensibles a más de 80,000 residentes de Providence y sus alrededores. Los servicios incluyen: medicina familiar, medicina interna, pediatría, obstetricia, ginecología, salud conductual, optometría, atención dental y atención exprés (atención de urgencia) junto con servicios especializados seleccionados. De estos servicios, varios practicantes y especialistas hablan español. Los médicos están certificados por la junta. Llame con anticipación para hacer una cita, no aceptan visitas sin cita previa. Aceptan muchas formas de seguro y, si no tiene seguro, pueden ayudarlo a solicitar un seguro de salud u ofrecerle una tarifa variable según el tamaño de la familia y los ingresos familiares. Traiga comprobante de ingresos para aplicar su visita bajo la escala móvil de tarifas.
English Translation
Providence Community Health Centers (PCHC) is a non-profit health care organization and the only Federally Qualified Health Center (FQHC) in Providence, Rhode Island. PCHC provides quality primary health care services that are affordable, comprehensive and culturally sensitive to more than 80,000 residents of Providence and its surrounding areas. Services include: Family Practice, Internal Medicine, Pediatrics, Obstetrics, Gynecology, Behavioral Health, Optometry, Dental Care and Express Care (urgent care) alongside with selected specialty services. Of these services, several practicioners and specialists speak Spanish. Physicians are board certified. Call ahead to make an appointment, they don't take walk-ins. They take many forms of insurance and if you are uninsured, they can assist you in applying for health insurance or offer a sliding fee based on family size and family income. Bring proof of income in order to apply your visit under the sliding fee scale.

At Lifespan Urgent Care in Providence, RI, our providers treat adults and children 18 months and older for common illnesses or injuries, including, but not limited to: Colds and flu, earaches and sinus infections, fractures and sprains, animal bites, cuts, stomach upset and diarrhea, burns, insect bites, urinary tract infections.
